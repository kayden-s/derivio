Probability Formula (Black-Scholes d2)

d2 = (ln(S/K) - 0.5 × σ² × T) / (σ × √T)

Call P(ITM) = N(d2) | Put P(ITM) = N(-d2)

Where: S = price, K = strike, σ = IV, T = time in years
