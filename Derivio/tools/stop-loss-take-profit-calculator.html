Risk/Reward Formulas

Max Risk = |Entry - Stop Loss| × Position Size

Max Reward = |Take Profit - Entry| × Position Size

R-Multiple = Reward ÷ Risk

Breakeven Win Rate = 1 ÷ (1 + R-Multiple)
