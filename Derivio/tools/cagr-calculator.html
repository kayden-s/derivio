CAGR Formula (Compound Annual Growth Rate)

CAGR = (Ending / Starting)^(1 / Years) - 1

Total Return = (Ending / Starting) - 1

Growth Multiple = Ending / Starting

Where Starting = initial value, Ending = final value, Years = time period
